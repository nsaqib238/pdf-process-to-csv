## Possible Reasons for Missing Tables

### 1. Image-Based Tables (Cannot be extracted programmatically)
Some tables in standards documents are scanned images or embedded graphics. Our pipeline can only extract text-based tables.

**Likely image-based candidates:**
- Tables with complex layouts (H1, H2, I1, I2)
- Tables with special symbols or diagrams
- Tables with merged/complex cell structures

### 2. Text-Based Lists (Rejected as "clause-shaped")
Some "tables" in standards are actually formatted lists that look tabular but are structurally prose.

**Likely rejected as clause-shaped:**
- Tables 3.1, 3.3, 3.4 (cable specifications - may be formatted as lists)
- Tables 4.1, 4.2, 4.3 (requirements lists)
- Tables 5.1, 5.2 (sizing guides)

### 3. Multi-Column Text Layouts (Rejected by 2-column prose detection)
Our Iteration 1 improvements specifically filter 2-column change lists and TOC entries, which might be overly aggressive.

### 4. Tables Without "Table X" Caption
Some tables might not have explicit "Table X" captions or have captions formatted differently.

### 5. Low Quality Tables (Below confidence threshold)
Tables with poor structure, low fill ratios, or high noise might be filtered by quality scoring.
